## Service stakeholders and stakeholder mapping
The following subsections summarise the key user, internal, and external stakeholders identified as playing a role in [Dairy](#dairy), [Meat](#meat), and [Wine](#wine) inspections.

For each stakeholder, their primary role in relation to inspections is listed. Their influence and/or objective in relation to the corresponding inspection is also shown. This information helps identify leading and supporting players in the inspection service. It also helps identify where stakeholders are contributors and/or consumers of the service.

Stakeholders who support or have an interest in more than one inspection area are marked with an asterisk(*).

See also [User Segments & Personas](#user-segments--personas).

## User Segments & Personas

The user segments identified for targeted research in this discovery cover the roles and/or teams involved in the delivery of core inspection work. 

* Full time inspectors
* Part time / assistant inspectors 
* Field Veterinary Coordinators (UAI only)
* Inspection Team Leads

These user roles are directly involved in tasks spanning planning, scheduling and preparing for inspections, conducting them, issuing enforcements and reporting on visits. They also encompass technical or team leadership and coordination.

These are the main candidates for user research. Research observations are synthesised into Personas

[Personas](personas-and-user-needs#personas) for priority user segments are further elaborated with [user needs](personas-and-user-needs#user-needs).
