## <a name="recommendation-1">Define customer relationship and strategy</a>

### Goals 

*"consistent user experiences"*

* set a clear tone 
* define common approach 
* clear steer for service development programme

### Constraints 

* Not just inspections - spans value chain
* Include FBOs, supply chain and agency partners

### Notes

* **Define expectations and positioning**
  * inbound and outbound 
* **Informs strategy and approach for**
  * CRM and case management 
  * contact channels (systems and operations)
  * gathering and maintaining customer intelligence
  * FBO profiles 
  * tone and delivery of guidance 

## <a name="recommendation-2">Appoint Head of Inspections Delivery</a>

### Goals 

**"provide service leadership and ownership"*

* strategic role
* decision-making authority
* across inspection areas

### Constraints 

* existing operational silos and leadership structure

### Notes

Key responsibilities 

- communicate clear service vision
- implement customer strategy
- lead service transformation programme
- ensure operational alignment
- support technical leadership (including MOC / operational guidance)
- ‘product own’ inspections platforms


## <a name="recommendation-3">Map out programme priorities in strategic context</a>

### Goals 

*"coherent service development strategy”"* 

* Keep mission focus and clarity across long-term programme
* Transform, don’t disrupt

### Constraints 

* Variable digital maturity 
* Tactical pressures

### Notes

* Align backlog priorities to 
  * vision operating model 
  * transformation review 
  * customer strategy
* Define prioritisation principles
  * capacity and dependencies
  * consistency and fairly
* Develop and communicate the story of change 
  * set expectations
  * share progress and impact
