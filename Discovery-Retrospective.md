## To improve

- Timing & engagement of key user groups when we're going to need their close cooperation - most were happy to make it happen but we also got push back
- We have a core set of really good activities for an 8 week disco now, just some fine-tuning around sequencing and allowing time to breathe (just not in anyone's face).
- Being able to analyse before creating assets.
- Documenting as we go - room to breathe. I have paid for lack of note clean up time when it got to analysis
- Wiki write up, feels like we never leave enough breathing room for it and it's a tricky task to handle / tool to wield at a point where we are all knackered / need something simpler
- Having the service assessment in the last week means a loss of time for analysis and synthesis, and fleshing out/creaming off recommendations.
- balance of talking v discussion in show and tell
- Consistency of weeknotes
- Clarity of roles and individual areas/outputs. Maybe spend a bit of time up front as a team to get everyone's input and make a collective outline plan so everyone feels involved and comfortable.
